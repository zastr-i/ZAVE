Members get first access to new merchandise, exclusive choreo drops, and invites to closed-door sessions. Take your spot.

Can I switch between membership tiers?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect the next billing cycle — just contact our studio team.

Are there any annual commitment fees?

No hidden fees. All memberships are month-to-month with no long-term lock-in, unless you choose our annual prepay option (contact for details).

Do members get priority for sold-out events?

Yes — Premium and All-Access members enjoy early booking windows and reserved spots for high-demand classes and events.

What if I'm new to dance?

Essential membership is perfect for beginners, but all levels are welcome. Our coaches adapt to your journey.
